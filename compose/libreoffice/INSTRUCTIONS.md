# LibreOffice Setup Instructions

## 1. Overview
[LibreOffice](https://docs.linuxserver.io/images/docker-libreoffice/#docker-compose-recommended-click-here-for-more-info) is a free and powerful office suite, and a successor to OpenOffice.org (commonly known as OpenOffice). Its clean interface and feature-rich tools help you unleash your creativity and enhance your productivity.

## 2. Prerequisites
- Default port: `3000`
