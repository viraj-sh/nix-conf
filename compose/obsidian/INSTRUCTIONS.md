# Obsidian Setup Instructions

## 1. Overview
[Obsidian](https://docs.linuxserver.io/images/docker-obsidian/#docker-compose-recommended-click-here-for-more-info) is a note-taking app that lets you create, link, and organize your notes on your device, with hundreds of plugins and themes to customize your workflow. 

## 2. Prerequisites
- Default port: `3000`
